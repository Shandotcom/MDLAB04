Siquijor

More about Siquijor

Overview

Siquijor is a small island province in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines known for its beautiful beaches, waterfalls, and peaceful island atmosphere. Often called the “Mystical Island,” it is famous for its local healing traditions, folklore, and natural attractions that attract visitors from different parts of the country and the world.

Location

Siquijor is located in the Central Visayas region, southeast of Negros Island and near the islands of Bohol and Cebu. Although it is one of the smallest provinces in the Philippines, the island is rich in natural beauty, scenic coastal views, and relaxing destinations.

Popular Attractions

  • Cambugahay Falls – A famous multi-tier waterfall with clear turquoise water where visitors can swim and enjoy rope swings.
  • Salagdoong Beach – A well-known beach with cliff-diving platforms and crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming.
  • Paliton Beach – A beautiful white-sand beach popular for relaxing and watching stunning sunsets.
  • And many more!

Island Experience

Visitors travel to Siquijor to enjoy nature, explore waterfalls and beaches, go snorkeling or diving, and experience the calm and friendly island lifestyle. The island is also known for its scenic coastal roads, lush greenery, and breathtaking sunset views, making it a great destination for both relaxation and adventure.
